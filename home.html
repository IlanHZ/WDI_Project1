Poker Dice

Rules

You can throw the dice three times.
Before you roll the dice, you can hold as many of them as you want to get the best score!

If you are happy with your hand before the end of the three times, just press 'stick'.

The player with the best of the following hands wins:

  • Nothing
  • One pair
  • Two pairs
  • Three of a kind
  • Small straight
  • Big straight
  • Full House
  • Five of a kind
