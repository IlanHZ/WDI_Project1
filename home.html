The player with the best of the following hands wins:

  • Nothing
  • One pair
  • Two paires
  • Three of a kind
  • Small straight
  • Big straight
  • Full House
  • Five of a kind

You can throw the dice 3 times. Before you roll the dice, you can hold as many of them as you want to get the best score! If you are happy with your hand before the end of the three times, just press 'stick'.
